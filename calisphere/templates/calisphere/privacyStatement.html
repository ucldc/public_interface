Privacy Statement

The California Digital Library (CDL) values your privacy. For a detailed description of what information we collect from visitors to this website and how it is used and managed, please see the CDL Online Privacy Notice.

Addendum

The following additional practices are specific to the administration of Calisphere.

Sharing information with content contributors

The content on this website is contributed by cultural heritage institutions across California. We provide our contributors aggregate reports of usage statistics on the content they have submitted, currently derived from web server logs.

Questions about items and collections should be directed to their contributing institutions. Use the "Contact Owner" button or visit the institutionâ€™s page for more information. Messages about items or collections submitted through Calisphere's Contact Us page may be forwarded to the contributing institution. Your contact information will be included so staff at the contributing institution may follow up with you directly if needed.

Third party services and APIs

Certain features on this website utilize third party services and APIs such as third party hosting of common JavaScript libraries and web fonts, and features such as threaded comments. Information used by an external service is governed by the privacy policy of that service. CDL does not control how information may be used by these services.

User surveys

This website will sometimes conduct user surveys. When doing so, it may set a flash local storage "cookie" so users are only surveyed once per computer.

Revisions

This statement was last revised on March 14, 2024 and may be revised at any time.
