Contact Calisphere

Please note that Calisphere does not own the items or collections on the site and has no further information about them. Questions about items and collections should be directed to their contributing institutions. Use the "contact owner" button or visit the institution's page for more information. If you have a question about using an item you found on Calisphere, you may want to first consult the Terms of Use. Other frequently asked questions are addressed on our help page.
